Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work? +

Download and install Nano Tracker. You have 15 days to try all features with a local database. No credit card required. After the trial, purchase a license to keep using it.

Can I use the same license on Mac and Windows? +

Yes. The Solo license covers 1 computer (Mac or Windows). The Studio license covers 5 computers, even mixed Mac and Windows.

What happens if I change computers? +

You can deactivate the license on your old PC directly from the app Settings and reactivate it on the new one. No support contact needed.

What's the difference between Solo and Studio? +

Solo uses a local database on your PC. Studio unlocks shared MariaDB: all studio PCs see the same disks and can search each other's files. Perfect for teams of 2-5 people.

What payment methods do you accept? +

Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay and Google Pay. Payments are securely handled by Lemon Squeezy.
